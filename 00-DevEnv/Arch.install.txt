#1 INTERNET
Ethernet:
	$ ip -a
	$ ping 1.1.1.1
	$ ip link set eno1 down (down ethernet)

Wifi:
	$ iwctl device list (list availabel wifi)
	$ iwctl device wlan0 set-property Powered off/on
	$ iwctl station wlan0 scan (scan for network)
	$ iwctl station wlan0 get-networks (list the scan networks)
	$ iwctl station wlan0 connect <network_name>
	$ iwctl station wlan0 show
